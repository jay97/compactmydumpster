How does your compactor work?

Our trucks are equipped with a 3 ton rotating compactor drum, the heaviest in the industry.

We use a truck-mounted compactor to bring our service your site. The drum is mounted on a hydraulic arm that moves over the trash. The operator controls the drum from inside the cab of truck using a joystick and video monitors. Custom-designed drum guards prevent the drum from damaging the dumpster.

With a short wheelbase, our trucks can navigate almost any job site. For peace of mind, we record every compacting job, so you can see results for yourself.
