Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2.25.2025

  1. Introduction

    Tampa Bay Mobile Trash Compactors, LLC (dba Compact My Dumpster) ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information, including data collected through our SMS messaging service.

  2. Information We Collect

    • Personal Information: Name, phone number, email, service preferences.
    • Usage Data: SMS interactions, service requests, and website visits.
    • Technical Data: Device information, IP address, and cookies for analytics.

  3. How We Use Your Information

    We use your information to:

    • Provide and improve our services
    • Send SMS notifications related to services, promotions, and alerts
    • Process transactions and customer inquiries
    • Ensure compliance with legal requirements

  4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

    We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share data with:

    • Service providers assisting in message delivery and operations
    • Law enforcement or regulatory agencies if required by law
    • Business partners for operational purposes (with safeguards)

  5. SMS Messaging & Communications

    Your consent to receive SMS messages includes service updates, appointment reminders, and promotional content. You can opt out anytime by replying STOP to any message.

  6. Data Retention & Security

    We retain data for as long as necessary for service operations or legal compliance. We use encryption, firewalls, and access controls to protect your information.

  7. Your Rights

    You may:

    • Request access to your personal data
    • Update or correct inaccuracies in your data
    • Opt-out of SMS messaging at any time

  8. Changes to This Policy

    We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on our website.

  9. Contact Us

    For questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: Hunter Bujnoski - hunter@compactmydumpster.com
